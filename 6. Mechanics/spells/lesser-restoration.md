# Lesser Restoration
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be [blinded](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#blinded), [deafened](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#deafened), [paralyzed](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#paralyzed), or [poisoned](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#poisoned).
