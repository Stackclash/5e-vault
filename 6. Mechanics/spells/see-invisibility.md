# See Invisibility
*2nd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a pinch of talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, you see [invisible](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#invisible) creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.

**Classes**: [Bard](/6. Mechanics/classes/bard.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](/6. Mechanics/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](/6. Mechanics/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer](/6. Mechanics/classes/sorcerer.md), [Wizard](/6. Mechanics/classes/wizard.md)

Source: Player's Handbook p. 274
