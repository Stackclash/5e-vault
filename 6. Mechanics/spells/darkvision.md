# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has [darkvision](/6. Mechanics/rules/senses.md#darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.
