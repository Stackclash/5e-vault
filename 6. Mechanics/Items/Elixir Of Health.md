# Elixir of Health
*Potion, minor, rare*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

When you drink this potion, it cures any disease afflicting you, and it removes the [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#blinded|blinded]], [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#deafened|deafened]], [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#paralyzed|paralyzed]], and [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#poisoned|poisoned]] conditions. The clear red liquid has tiny bubbles of light in it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 168*
