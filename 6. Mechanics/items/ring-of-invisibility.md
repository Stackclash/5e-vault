# Ring of Invisibility
*Ring, major, legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Requires Attunement](/6. Mechanics/rules/item-properties.md#Requires%20Attunement)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing this ring, you can turn [invisible](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#invisible) as an action. Anything you are wearing or carrying is [invisible](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#invisible) with you. You remain [invisible](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#invisible) until the ring is removed, until you attack or cast a spell, or until you use a bonus action to become visible again.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 191*
