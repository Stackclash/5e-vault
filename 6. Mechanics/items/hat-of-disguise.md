# Hat of Disguise
*Wondrous Item, major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Requires Attunement](/6. Mechanics/rules/item-properties.md#Requires%20Attunement)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While wearing this hat, you can use an action to cast the [disguise self](/6. Mechanics/spells/disguise-self.md) spell from it at will. The spell ends if the hat is removed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 173*
