# Ring of Free Action
*Ring, major, rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Requires Attunement](/6. Mechanics/rules/item-properties.md#Requires%20Attunement)
- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

While you wear this ring, difficult terrain doesn't cost you extra movement. In addition, magic can neither reduce your speed nor cause you to be [paralyzed](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#paralyzed) or [restrained](/6. Mechanics/rules/conditions.md#restrained).

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 191*
