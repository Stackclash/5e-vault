# See Invisibility
*2nd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a pinch of talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, you see [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#invisible|invisible]] creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.
