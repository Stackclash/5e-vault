# Greater Invisibility
*4th-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You or a creature you touch becomes [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#invisible|invisible]] until the spell ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#invisible|invisible]] as long as it is on the target's person.
