# Power Word Heal
*9th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of healing energy washes over the creature you touch. The target regains all its hit points. If the creature is [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#charmed\|charmed]], [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#frightened\|frightened]], [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#paralyzed\|paralyzed]], or [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#stunned\|stunned]], the condition ends. If the creature is [[6. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#prone\|prone]], it can use its reaction to stand up. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

**Classes**: [[/6. Mechanics/Classes/Bard.md\|Bard]], [[/6. Mechanics/Classes/Sorcerer Divine Soul (XGE).md\|Sorcerer (Divine Soul)]]

Source: Player's Handbook p. 266
