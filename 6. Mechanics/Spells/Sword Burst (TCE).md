# Sword Burst
*cantrip, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (5-feet radius)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You create a momentary circle of spectral blades that sweep around you. All other creatures within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take `dice: 1d6` (`1d6`) force damage.

This spell's damage increases by `dice: 1d6` (`1d6`) when you reach 5th level (`dice: 2d6` (`2d6`)), 11th level (`dice: 3d6` (`3d6`)), and 17th level (`dice: 4d6` (`4d6`)).
