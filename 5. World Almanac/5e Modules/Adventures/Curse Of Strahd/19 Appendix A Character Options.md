# Appendix A: Character Options
*Source: Curse of Strahd, p. 209* 

## Character Background

During character creation, players can select the following background for their characters, with your approval: [[Haunted One%20\|(VRGR]].md). It is appropriate for any character or campaign associated with eeriness or horror.

## Gothic Trinkets

When rolling for a trinket, consider using the [[Trinket \|(COS]].md), which is designed for a gothic game.
