| dice: d8 | Cult |

| 6 | A cult of [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Npc/Dyrrn (ERLW).md\|Dyrrn]] led by a [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Aberration/Mind Flayer.md\|mind flayer]] kidnaps people with dragonmarks, seeking to decipher the marks and transfer them to others. |
