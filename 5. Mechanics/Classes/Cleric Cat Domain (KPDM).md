## Class Features

### Cat Domain (Level 1)

You embody the grace, strength, and resilience of felines. Eventually, you become able to transform physically into a lion or tiger.

### A Claw in the Clowder (Level 1)

On choosing this domain at 1st level, you learn the true strike cantrip and you gain proficiency in the following skills: Acrobatics and Stealth.

### Channel Divinity: Feline Finesse (Level 2)

At 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to add a +10 bonus to any Dexterity ability or skill check made by you or someone you designate within 30 feet.

### Eyes of the Cat (Level 6)

Beginning at 6th level, you gain [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]] with a range of 60 feet. If you already have [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]], the range extends to 90 feet.

### Divine Strike (Level 8)

Beginning at 6th level, you gain [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]] with a range of 60 feet. If you already have [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]], the range extends to 90 feet.

### Emissary of the Cat (Level 17)

At 17th level, you become a natural lycanthrope of the weretiger or werelion type, whichever is appropriate to your deity. See 'Player Characters as Lycanthropes' in the standard rules on werewolves for details. You cannot spread the disease of lycanthropy.
