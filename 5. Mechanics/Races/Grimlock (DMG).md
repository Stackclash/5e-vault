# Grimlock (DMG)
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 282*  

- **Ability Scores**: Strength +2, Charisma -2
- **Type**: race
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft.

## Traits

### Blindsight

You have no eyes, and cannot be [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#blinded|blinded]]. You can perceive your surroundings within 30 ft., or 10 ft. while [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#deafened|deafened]]. You are considered blind beyond this radius.

### Keen Hearing and Smell

You have advantage on Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Perception|")"]] checks that rely on hearing or smell.

### Stone Camouflage

You have advantage on Dexterity ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Stealth|")"]] checks made to hide in rocky terrain.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write Undercommon.
