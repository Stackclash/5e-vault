# Simil (Clarity)
*Source: Deep Magic p. 179*  

The user adds half your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1) to its Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Insight\|Insight]]) and Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Perception\|Perception]]) checks for 1 minute. *Greater:* The user gains [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Blindsight\|blindsight]] out to a range of 10 feet.
