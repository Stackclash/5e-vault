# Thirsting Blade
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the SRD.* 

You can attack with your pact weapon twice, instead of once, whenever you take the [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Actions.md#Attack\|Attack]] action on your turn.
