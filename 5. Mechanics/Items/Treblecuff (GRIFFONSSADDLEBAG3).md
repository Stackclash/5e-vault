# Treblecuff
*Wondrous item, minor, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag3/Treblecuff.webp#right)  


While wearing this ear cuff, you can add half your proficiency bonus to any ability check you make to play an instrument if you aren't proficient with it. In addition, you can magically discern (and consequently recreate) the note of any sound you hear.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 3 p. 0*
