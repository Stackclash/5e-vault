# Staff of Frost
*Staff, major, very rare (requires attunement by a druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 B
  - Two-handed: 1d8 B
- **Properties**: Requires Attunement, [[\5. Mechanics\Rules\Item Properties.md#Versatile|Versatile]]
- **Cost**: ‚è§
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

You have resistance to cold damage while you hold this staff.

The staff has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC: [[5. Mechanics\Spells\Cone Of Cold.md|cone of cold]] (5 charges), [[5. Mechanics\Spells\Fog Cloud.md|fog cloud]] (1 charge), [[5. Mechanics\Spells\Ice Storm.md|ice storm]] (4 charges), or [[5. Mechanics\Spells\Wall Of Ice.md|wall of ice]] (4 charges).

The staff regains `dice: 1d6 + 4` (`1d6 + 4`) expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a `dice: d20` (`d20`). On a 1. the staff turns to water and is destroyed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 202. Available in the SRD.*
