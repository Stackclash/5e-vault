# Dragon Slayer
*Major, rare*  
![[5. Mechanics/Items/img/dragon-slayer.webp#right]]  


You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When you hit a dragon with this weapon, the dragon takes an extra `dice:3d6|noform|avg` (`3d6`) damage of the weapon's type. For the purpose of this weapon, "dragon" refers to any creature with the dragon type, including dragon turtles and wyverns.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 166. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
