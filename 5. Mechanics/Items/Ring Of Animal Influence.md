# Ring of Animal Influence
*Ring, major, rare*  
![[5. Mechanics/Items/img/ring-of-animal-influence.jpg#right]]  


This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `dice: 1d3|avg` (`1d3`) expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast one of the following spells:

- [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Animal Friendship.md\|Animal friendship]] (save DC 13)  
- [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Fear.md\|Fear]] (save DC 13), targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or lower  
- [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Speak With Animals.md\|Speak with animals]]  

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 189. Available in the SRD.*
