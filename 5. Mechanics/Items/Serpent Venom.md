# Serpent Venom
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  

- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Poison\|Poison]]
- **Cost**: 200 gp

This poison must be harvested from a dead or [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Incapacitated\|incapacitated]] [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Beast/Giant Poisonous Snake.md\|giant poisonous snake]]. A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(10)` (`3d6`) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
