# Barbed Cloak
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  

- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Crafted%20by\|Crafted by]]

While you are wearing this cloak, you deal `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) piercing damage to any creature grappling you at the start of your turn.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 120*
