# Elemental Gem, Emerald
*Wondrous Item, minor, uncommon*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

This gem contains a mote of elemental energy. When you use an action to break the gem, a [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Elemental/Water Elemental.md|water elemental]] is summoned as if you had cast the [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Conjure Elemental.md|conjure elemental]] spell, and the gem's magic is lost.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 167. Available in the SRD.*
