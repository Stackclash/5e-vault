# Viper Strike
*Generic Variant, varies, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this weapon, you deal an additional `dice: 10|avg` (`10`) poison damage, or `dice: 14|avg` (`14`) poison damage if you are using the 'very rare' version of this weapon. If you are using the 'very rare' version of this weapon, you also gain a +1 to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon.
