# Mithral Armor
*Generic Variant, minor, uncommon*  


Mithral is a light, flexible metal. A mithral chain shirt or breastplate can be worn under normal clothes. If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Stealth\|Stealth]]) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn't.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 182. Available in the SRD.*
