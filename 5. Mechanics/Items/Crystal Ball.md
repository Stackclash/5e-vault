# Crystal Ball
*Wondrous item, major, very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This crystal ball is about 6 inches in diameter. While touching it, you can cast the [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Scrying.md\|scrying]] spell (save DC 17) with it.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 159. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
