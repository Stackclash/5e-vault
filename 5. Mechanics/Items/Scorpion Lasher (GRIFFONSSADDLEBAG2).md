# Scorpion Lasher
*Weapon ([[5. Mechanics/Items/Whip.md\|whip]]), major, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag2/Items/Scorpion-Lasher.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d4 S
- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Finesse\|Finesse]], [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Reach\|Reach]]
- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This whip was constructed using salvaged pieces of a giant scorpion and drips with a sickly venom. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with this weapon, you can cause the whip to deal an extra `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) poison damage. When you do, if the creature is Medium or smaller, it must also succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be pulled 5 feet closer to you.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 2 p. 155*
