# Cloak of the Scorched Martyr
*Wondrous Item, legendary, Crafted by (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

While wearing and attuned to this cloak, you have a +1 bonus to your AC, resistance to fire damage, and whenever you hit with a weapon attack, you may deal an additional `dice: 2d10|avg` (`2d10`) fire damage to your target. In addition, you may use your action to target one creature you can see within 120 feet of you and command them to combust; that target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or take `dice: 4d10|avg` (`4d10`) fire damage.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 84*
