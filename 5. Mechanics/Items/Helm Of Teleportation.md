# Helm of Teleportation
*Wondrous item, major, rare (requires attunement)*  
![[5. Mechanics/Items/img/helm-of-teleportation.webp#right]]  


This helm has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the [[5. Mechanics/Spells/Teleport.md\|teleport]] spell from it. The helm regains `dice:1d3|noform|noparens|avg` (`d3`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 174. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
