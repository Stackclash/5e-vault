# Remorhaz Javelin
*Weapon ([[5. Mechanics/Items/Javelin.md\|javelin]]), major, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag3/Remorhaz-Javelin.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d6 P
- **Range**: 30/120
- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Thrown\|Thrown]]
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

This javelin is decorated with the frill spines and teeth of a slain remorhaz. Targets hit by this magic weapon take an extra `dice:1d4|noform|avg` (`1d4`) damage. The type of bonus damage is cold if the total of the attack roll is even, or fire damage if the total is odd.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 3 p. 0*
