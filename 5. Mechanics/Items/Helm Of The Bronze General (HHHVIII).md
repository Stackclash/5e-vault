# Helm of the Bronze General
*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Crafted%20by\|Crafted by]]

While wearing this helm you have a +2 bonus to AC and you may use your bonus action to appear dreadful until the start of your next turn, forcing each creature that starts their turn within 10 feet of you to succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or be [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Frightened\|frightened]] until the start of their next turn.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 90*
