# Potion of Adult Dragonstrength
*Potion, very rare*  

- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Crafted%20by\|Crafted by]]

When you drink this potion, you recover `dice:10d4+10|noform|avg` (`10d4 + 10`) hit points. In addition, you gain resistance to the damage type of the dragon it was crafted from for 1 hour.
