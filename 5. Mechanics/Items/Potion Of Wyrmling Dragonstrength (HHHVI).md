# Potion of Wyrmling Dragonstrength
*Potion, uncommon*  

- **Properties**: [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Crafted%20by\|Crafted by]]

When you drink this potion, you recover `dice:3d4+3|noform|avg` (`3d4 + 3`) hit points. In addition, you gain resistance to the damage type of the dragon it was crafted from for 1 hour.
