# Trickster's Hammer
*Melee Weapon, very rare, Crafted by (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d8 B
  - Two-handed: 1d10 B
- **Properties**: Crafted by, Martial, [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Item Properties.md#Versatile\|Versatile]]
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. When you make a successful critical hit against a creature with this weapon, you add a random extra effect to your attack. Roll a `dice: d4|avg|noform` (`d4`) and consult the table below for your result:

**Effect**

`dice: [[Tricksters Hammer.md#^effect]]`

| dice: d4 | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | You deal `dice: 1d8\|avg\|noform` (`1d8`) necrotic damage and gain temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt (this damage is not doubled). |
| 2 | You deal an additional `dice: 2d8\|avg\|noform` (`2d8`) lightning damage to your target (this damage is not doubled). |
| 3 | Your target must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or become [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#stunned\|stunned]] until the end of your next turn. |
| 4 | You teleport, along with any equipment you are wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. |
