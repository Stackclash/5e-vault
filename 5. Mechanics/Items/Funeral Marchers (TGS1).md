# Funeral Marchers
*Wondrous item, major, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew/master/_img/TGS1/Funeral-Marchers.webp#right)  


While wearing these black leather boots, you can choose to not fall [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Prone\|prone]] when you're reduced to 0 hit points. While you're [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Unconscious\|unconscious]] and standing in this way, you can walk up to 10 feet on your turn after rolling a death saving throw.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 1 p. 70*
