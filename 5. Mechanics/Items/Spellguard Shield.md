# Spellguard Shield
*Armor ([[5. Mechanics/Items/Shield.md\|shield]]), major, very rare (requires attunement)*  
![[5. Mechanics/Items/img/spellguard-shield.webp#right]]  

- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

While holding this shield, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects, and spell attacks have disadvantage against you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 201. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
