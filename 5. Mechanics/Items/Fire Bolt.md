# Fire Bolt
*Ammunition, uncommon, Crafted by*  

- **Damage**: 1d6 F
- **Properties**: Crafted by
- **Weight**: 0.075 lbs.

These bolts combust upon direct piercing organic material. When you hit a target successfully with this bolt, you deal an additional `dice: 1d6|avg` (`1d6`) fire damage. These bolts lose this property on hit and become regular bolts afterwards.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 127*
