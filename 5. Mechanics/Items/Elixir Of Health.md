# Elixir of Health
*Potion, minor, rare*  

- **Cost**: ⏤
- **Weight**: ⏤

When you drink this potion, it cures any disease afflicting you, and it removes the [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#blinded\|blinded]], [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#deafened\|deafened]], [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#paralyzed\|paralyzed]], and [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#poisoned\|poisoned]] conditions.
