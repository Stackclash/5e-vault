# Revenant Blade
*Source: Eberron: Rising from the Last War p. 22*  

***Prerequisites*** [[5. Mechanics/Races/Elf.md\|Elf]]

You are descended from a master of the [[5. Mechanics/Items/Double Bladed Scimitar (ERLW).md\|double-bladed scimitar]], and some of that mastery has passed on to you. You gain the following benefits:

- While you are holding a double-bladed scimitar with two hands, you gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class.  
- A double-bladed scimitar has the finesse property when you wield it.
