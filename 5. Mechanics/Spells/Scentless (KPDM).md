# Scentless
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, 1 ounce of pure water
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a willing creature or object that is not being worn or carried. For the duration, the target gives off no odor. A creature that relies on smell has disadvantage on Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Perception\|Perception]]) checks to detect the target and Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Survival\|Survival]]) checks to track the target. The target is [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Invisible\|invisible]] to a creature that relies solely on smell to sense its surroundings. This spell has no effect on targets with unusually strong scents, such as [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Undead/Ghast.md\|ghasts]].
