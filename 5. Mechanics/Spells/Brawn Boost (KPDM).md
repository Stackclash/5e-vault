# Brawn Boost
*cantrip, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You touch a willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a `dice:d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) and add the result to one Strength check of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the check. The spell then ends.
