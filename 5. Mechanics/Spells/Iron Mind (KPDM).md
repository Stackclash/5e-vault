# Iron Mind
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

One willing creature you touch becomes immune to psychic damage and mind-altering effects for the spell's duration.
