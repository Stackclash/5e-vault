# Elemental Twist
*2nd-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a thin piece of copper twisted around itself)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

During this spell's duration, reality shifts around you whenever you cast a spell that deals acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder damage. Assign each damage type a number and roll a `dice:d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) to determine the type of damage this casting of the spell deals. In addition, the spell's damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`). All other properties or effects of the spell are unchanged.
