# Memento Mori
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 5 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You transform yourself into a horrifying vision of death, rotted and crawling with maggots, exuding the stench of the grave. Each creature within range that can see you must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Stunned\|stunned]] until the end of its next turn.

A creature that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to further castings of this spell for 24 hours.
