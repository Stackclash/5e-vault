# Wind Lash
*cantrip, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 20 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Your swift gesture creates a solid lash of howling wind. Make a melee spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) slashing damage from the shearing wind and is pushed 5 feet away from you.

The spell's damage increases by `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) when you reach 5th level (`dice:2d8|noform|avg` (`2d8`)), 11th level (`dice:3d8|noform|avg` (`3d8`)), and 17th level (`dice:4d8|noform|avg` (`4d8`)).
