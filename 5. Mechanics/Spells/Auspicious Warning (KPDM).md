# Auspicious Warning
*1st-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Just in time, you call out a fortunate warning to a creature within range. The target rolls a `dice:d4|noform|avg` (`d4`) and adds the number rolled to an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw that it has just made and uses the new result for determining success or failure.
