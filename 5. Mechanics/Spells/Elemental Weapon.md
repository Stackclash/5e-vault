# Elemental Weapon
*3rd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

A nonmagical weapon you touch becomes a magic weapon. Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. For the duration, the weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`d4`) damage of the chosen type when it hits.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th or 6th level, the bonus to attack rolls increases to +2 and the extra damage increases to `dice:2d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(2d4)`. When you use a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3 and the extra damage increases to `dice:3d4|noform|noparens|avg|text(3d4)`.
