# Draconic Senses
*4th-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You enhance your senses, granting yourself the keen perception of a dragon. For the duration of the spell, you gain [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]] out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Darkvision\|darkvision]], its range increases by 60 feet. You also gain [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Blindsight\|blindsight]] out to a range of 60 feet.
