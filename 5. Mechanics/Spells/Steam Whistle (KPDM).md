# Steam Whistle
*8th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Radius)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small brass whistle)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You open your mouth and unleash a shattering scream. All other creatures in a 30-foot radius around you take `dice:10d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`10d10`) thunder damage and are [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Deafened\|deafened]] for `dice:1d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`d8`) hours. A successful Constitution saving throw halves the damage and reduces the deafness to 1 round.
