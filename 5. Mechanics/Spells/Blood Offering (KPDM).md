# Blood Offering
*3rd-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You must have dealt damage to the creature before it died, and it must have been dead for no more than 1 hour.
