# Kobold's Fury
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a kobold scale
- **Duration:** 1 round

Your touch infuses the rage of a threatened kobold into the target. The target has advantage on melee weapon attacks until the end of its next turn. In addition, its next successful melee weapon attack against a creature larger than itself does an additional `dice:2d8|noform|avg` (`2d8`) damage.
