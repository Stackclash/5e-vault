# Decay
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a handful of ash
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you touch. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) necrotic damage. If the target is a Tiny or Small nonmagical object that isn't being worn or carried by a creature, it automatically takes maximum damage from the spell.

This spell's damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) when you reach 5th level (`dice:2d10|noform|avg` (`2d10`)), 11th level (`dice:3d10|noform|avg` (`3d10`)), and 17th level (`dice:4d10|noform|avg` (`4d10`)).
