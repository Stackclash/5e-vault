# Desiccating Breath
*4th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (30-feet cone)
- **Components:** V, S, a clump of dried clay
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You spew forth a cloud of black dust that draws all moisture from a 30-foot cone. Each animal in the cone takes `dice:4d10|noform|avg` (`4d10`) necrotic damage, or half as much damage if it makes a successful Constitution saving throw. The damage is `dice:6d10|noform|avg` (`6d10`) for plants and plant creatures, also halved on a successful Constitution saving throw.
