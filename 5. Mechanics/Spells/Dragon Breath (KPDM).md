# Dragon Breath
*5th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Special
- **Components:** V, S, a piece of a dragon's tooth
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You summon draconic power to gain a breath weapon. When you cast dragon breath, you can immediately exhale a 15-foot cone or a 30-foot line of elemental energy, depending on the type of dragon you select. While the spell remains active, roll a `dice:d6|noform|avg` (`d6`) at the start of your turn. On a roll of 5 or 6, you can take a bonus action that turn to use the breath weapon again.

When you cast the spell, choose one of the dragon types listed below. Your choice determines the affected area and the damage of the breath attack for the spell's duration.

**Dragon Type**

| Color | Area | Damage |
|-------|------|--------|
| Black | 30-foot line, 5 feet wide | `dice:6d6\|noform\|avg` (`6d6`) acid damage |
| Blue | 30-foot line, 5 feet wide | `dice:6d6\|noform\|avg` (`6d6`) lightning damage |
| Green | 15-foot cone | `dice:6d6\|noform\|avg` (`6d6`) poison damage |
| Red | 15-foot cone | `dice:6d6\|noform\|avg` (`6d6`) fire damage |
| White | 15-foot cone | `dice:6d6\|noform\|avg` (`6d6`) cold damage |
^dragon-type

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:2d6|noform|avg|text(2d6)` for each slot level above 5th.
