# Forceful Repurposing
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You attempt to hijack a spell being cast and redirect it to serve your purpose. Make an ability check using your spellcasting ability against a DC of 13 + the spell's level. On a successful check, you choose the target or targets for the spell.

Your targets must be valid targets for the spell based on the caster's ability and the range of the spell, but using your distinctions for friendly and hostile creatures.
