# Reverberate
*2nd-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (15-feet cone)
- **Components:** V, S, a metal ring
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch the ground at your feet with the metal ring, creating an impact that shakes the earth ahead of you. Creatures and unattended objects touching the ground in a 15-foot cone emanating from you take `dice:4d6|noform|avg` (`4d6`) thunder damage, and creatures fall [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Prone\|prone]]; a creature that makes a successful Dexterity saving throw takes half the damage and does not fall [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Prone\|prone]].

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|avg|text(1d6)` for each slot level above 2nd.
