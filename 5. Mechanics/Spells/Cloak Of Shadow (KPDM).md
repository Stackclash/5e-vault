# Cloak of Shadow
*1st-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You cloak yourself in shadow, giving you advantage on Dexterity ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Stealth\|Stealth]]) checks against creatures that rely on sight.
