# Hamstring
*cantrip, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You create an arrow of eldritch energy and send it at a target you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d4|noform|avg` (`1d4`) force damage, and it can't take reactions until the end of its next turn.

The spell's damage increases by `dice:1d4|noform|avg` (`1d4`) when you reach 5th level (`dice:2d4|noform|avg` (`2d4`)), 11th level (`dice:3d4|noform|avg` (`3d4`)), and 17th level (`dice:4d4|noform|avg` (`4d4`)).
