# Thunderous Charge
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

With a thunderous battle cry, you move up to 10 feet in a straight line and make a melee weapon attack. If it hits, you can choose to either gain a +5 bonus on the attack's damage or [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Actions.md#Shove\|shove]] the target 10 feet.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the distance you can move increases by 10 feet, and the attack deals an additional `dice:1d6|noform|avg|text(1d6)` thunder damage, for each slot level above 1st.
