# Shadow Bite
*cantrip, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You create a momentary needle of cold, sharp pain in a creature within range. The target must make a successful Constitution saving throw or take `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) necrotic damage immediately and have its speed halved until the start of your next turn.

This spell's damage increases to `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) when you reach 5th level, `dice:3d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d6`) when you reach 11th level, and `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) when you reach 17th level.
