# Bloodhound
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of ammonia)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You touch a willing creature to grant it an enhanced sense of smell. For the duration, that creature has advantage on Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Perception\|Perception]]) checks that rely on smell and on Wisdom ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Survival\|Survival]]) checks to follow tracks.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you also grant the target [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Senses.md#Blindsight\|blindsight]] out to a range of 30 feet for the duration.
