# Inflict Wounds
*1st-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes `dice:3d10|noform|avg` (`3d10`) necrotic damage.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|avg|text(1d10)` for each slot level above 1st.
