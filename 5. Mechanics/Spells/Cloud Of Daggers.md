# Cloud of Daggers
*2nd-level, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a sliver of glass
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You fill the air with spinning daggers in a cube 5 feet on each side, centered on a point you choose within range. A creature takes `dice:4d4|noform|avg` (`4d4`) slashing damage when it enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:2d4|noform|avg|text(2d4)` for each slot level above 2nd.
