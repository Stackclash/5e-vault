# Power Word Pain
*4th-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a quill jabbed into your own body
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

When you utter this word of power, one creature within 60 feet of you takes `dice:4d10|noform|avg` (`4d10`) force damage. At the start of each of its turns, the creature must make a successful Constitution saving throw or take an extra `dice:4d10|noform|avg` (`4d10`) force damage. The effect ends on a successful save.
