# Animate Ghoul
*2nd-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a piece of rotting flesh and an onyx gemstone worth 100 gp
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You raise one Medium or Small humanoid corpse as a [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Undead/Ghoul.md\|ghoul]] under your control. Any class levels or abilities the creature had in life are gone, replaced by the standard [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Undead/Ghoul.md\|ghoul]] stat block.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a 3rd-level spell slot, it can be used on the corpse of a Large humanoid to create a Large [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Undead/Ghoul.md\|ghoul]]. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, this spell creates a [[5. Mechanics/Bestiary/Undead/Ghast.md\|ghast]], but the material component changes to an onyx gemstone worth at least 200 gp.
