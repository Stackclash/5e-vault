# Feather Field
*1st-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, fletching from an arrow
- **Duration:** 1 round

A magical barrier of chaff in the form of feathers appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC against ranged attacks by magic weapons.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the duration is increased by 1 round for each slot level above 1st.
