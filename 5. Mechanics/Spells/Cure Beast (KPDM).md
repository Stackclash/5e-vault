# Cure Beast
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A beast of your choice that you can see within range regains a number of hit points equal to `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) + your spellcasting modifier.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by `dice:1d6|noform|avg|text(1d6)` for each slot level above 1st.
