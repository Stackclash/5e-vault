# Snowy Coat
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

This spell makes a slight alteration to a target creature's appearance that gives it advantage on Dexterity ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Stealth\|Stealth]]) checks to hide in snowy terrain. In addition, the target can use a bonus action to make itself [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Invisible\|invisible]] in snowy terrain for 1 minute. The spell ends at the end of the minute or when the creature attacks or casts a spell.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
