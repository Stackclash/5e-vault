# Thunderous Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The first time you hit with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your weapon rings with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the attack deals an extra `dice:2d6|noform|avg` (`2d6`) thunder damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Prone\|prone]].

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 282*
