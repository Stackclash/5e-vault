# Frailty
*cantrip, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

You temporarily inhibit the vital force of a creature within range. If the target makes a successful Constitution saving throw, the spell is ineffective. On a failed save, the next time the creature makes a Constitution check before the spell ends, roll a `dice:d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`d6`) and subtract the result from the roll. The spell then ends.
