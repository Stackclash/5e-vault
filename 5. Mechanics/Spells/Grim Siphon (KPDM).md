# Grim Siphon
*1st-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, the skull of a Tiny mammal or bird
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You twist the forces of entropy around two creatures, bringing distress to one while staving off the demise of the other. A creature you can see within range must make a successful Constitution saving throw or take poison damage equal to `dice:1d4|noform|avg` (`1d4`) + your spellcasting ability modifier. Another creature you choose that is within 5 feet of the target regains hit points equal to half the amount of poison damage dealt.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the poison damage increases by `dice:1d4|noform|avg|text(1d4)` for each slot level above 1st.
