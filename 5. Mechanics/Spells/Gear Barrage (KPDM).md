# Gear Barrage
*2nd-level, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (60-feet cone)
- **Components:** V, S, a handful of gears and sprockets worth 5 gp
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You create a burst of magically propelled gears. Each creature within a 60-foot cone takes `dice:3d8|noform|avg` (`3d8`) slashing damage, or half as much damage with a successful Dexterity saving throw. Constructs have disadvantage on the saving throw.
