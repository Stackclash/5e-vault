# Primal Infusion
*5th-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a bit of fur from a carnivorous animal
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You channel the fury of nature, drawing on its power. Until the spell ends, you gain the following benefits:

- You gain 30 temporary hit points. If any of these remain when the spell ends, they are lost.  
- You have advantage on attack rolls when one of your allies is within 5 feet of the target and the ally isn't [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Incapacitated\|incapacitated]].  
- Your weapon attacks deal an extra `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) damage of the same type dealt by the weapon on a hit.  
- You gain a +2 bonus to AC.  
- You have proficiency on Constitution saving throws.  

**Classes**: [[List Spells Classes Bard\|Bard]]; [[List Spells Classes Ranger\|Ranger]]

*Source: Deep Magic p. 100*
