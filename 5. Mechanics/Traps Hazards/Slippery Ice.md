# Slippery Ice
*Wilderness Hazard*  

Slippery ice is difficult terrain. When a creature moves onto slippery ice for the first time on a turn, it must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity ([[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Skills.md#Acrobatics\|Acrobatics]]) check or fall [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Prone\|prone]].

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 110*
