# Boon of the Unfettered

You have advantage on ability checks made to resist being [[/5. Mechanics/Rules/Conditions.md#Grappled\|grappled]]. In addition, you can use an action to automatically escape a grapple or free yourself of restraints of any kind. 

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 232*
