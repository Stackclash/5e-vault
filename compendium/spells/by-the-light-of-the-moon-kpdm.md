# By the Light of the Moon
*2nd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (30-feet radius)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The area within 30 feet of you becomes bathed in magical moonlight. In addition to providing dim light, it highlights objects and locations that are hidden or that hold a useful clue. Until the spell ends, all Wisdom ([Perception](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) and Intelligence ([Investigation](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Investigation)) checks made in the area are made with advantage.
