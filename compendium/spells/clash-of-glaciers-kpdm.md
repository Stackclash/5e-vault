# Clash of Glaciers
*5th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (100-feet line)
- **Components:** V, S, a piece of cracked glass
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You conjure up icy boulders that crush creatures in a line 100 feet long. Each creature in the area takes `dice:5d6|noform|avg` (`5d6`) bludgeoning damage plus `dice:5d6|noform|avg` (`5d6`) cold damage, or half the damage with a successful Dexterity saving throw.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|avg|text(1d6)` for each slot level above 5th. You decide whether each extra die deals bludgeoning or cold damage.
