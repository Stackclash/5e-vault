# Elemental Twist
*2nd-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a thin piece of copper twisted around itself
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

During this spell's duration, reality shifts around you whenever you cast a spell that deals acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder damage. Assign each damage type a number and roll a `dice:d6|noform|avg` (`d6`) to determine the type of damage this casting of the spell deals. In addition, the spell's damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`). All other properties or effects of the spell are unchanged.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Sorcerer](list-spells-classes-sorcerer); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard); [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](list-spells-classes-rogue-arcane-trickster)
