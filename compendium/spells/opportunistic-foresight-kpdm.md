# Opportunistic Foresight
*3rd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You instill a creature you touch with the ability to predict and react to advantages in combat. For the duration of the spell, the target gains a bonus action each turn that it can use to make an [opportunity attack](/compendium/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) against a provoking opponent. The target gains advantage on any [opportunity attacks](/compendium/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) it takes while the spell is in effect.
