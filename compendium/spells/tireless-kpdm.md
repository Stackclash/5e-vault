# Tireless
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** S, an ever-wound spring worth 50 gp
- **Duration:** 24 hour

You grant machine-like stamina to a creature you touch for the duration of the spell. The target requires no food or drink or rest. It can move at three times its normal speed during overland travel and perform three times the usual amount of labor. The target is not protected from fatigue or [exhaustion](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) caused by a magical effect.
