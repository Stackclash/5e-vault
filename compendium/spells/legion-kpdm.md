# Legion
*3rd-level, Conjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a toy soldier
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You call down a legion of shadowy soldiers in a 10-foot cube. Their features resemble a mockery of once-living creatures. Whenever a creature starts its turn inside the cube or within 5 feet of it, or enters the cube for the first time on its turn, the conjured shades make an attack using your melee spell attack modifier; on a hit, the target takes `dice:3d8|noform|avg` (`3d8`) necrotic damage. The space inside the cube is considered difficult terrain.
