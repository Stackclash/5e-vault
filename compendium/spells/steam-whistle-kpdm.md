# Steam Whistle
*8th-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (30-feet radius)
- **Components:** V, S, a small brass whistle
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You open your mouth and unleash a shattering scream. All other creatures in a 30-foot radius around you take `dice:10d10|noform|avg` (`10d10`) thunder damage and are [deafened](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) for `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) hours. A successful Constitution saving throw halves the damage and reduces the deafness to 1 round.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Warlock](list-spells-classes-warlock); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard)

*Source: Deep Magic p. 109*
