# Draconic Senses
*4th-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You enhance your senses, granting yourself the keen perception of a dragon. For the duration of the spell, you gain [darkvision](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have [darkvision](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Darkvision), its range increases by 60 feet. You also gain [blindsight](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Blindsight) out to a range of 60 feet.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Sorcerer](list-spells-classes-sorcerer); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard); [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](list-spells-classes-rogue-arcane-trickster)

*Source: Deep Magic p. 200*
