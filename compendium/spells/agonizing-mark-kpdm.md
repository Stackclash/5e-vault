# Agonizing Mark
*1st-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You choose a creature you can see within range to mark as your prey, and a ray of black energy issues forth from you. Until the spell ends, each time you deal damage to the target it must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, it falls [prone](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Prone) as its body is filled with torturous agony.
