# Cloak of Shadow
*1st-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You cloak yourself in shadow, giving you advantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks against creatures that rely on sight.
