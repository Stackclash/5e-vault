# Arcane Sight
*8th-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a piece of clear quartz
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

The recipient of this spell gains the benefits of both [true seeing](compendium/spells/true-seeing.md) and [detect magic](compendium/spells/detect-magic.md) until the spell ends, and also knows the name and effect of every spell he or she witnesses during the spell's duration.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Sorcerer](list-spells-classes-sorcerer); [Warlock](list-spells-classes-warlock); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard)

*Source: Deep Magic p. 38*
