# Mire
*3rd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 100 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a vial of sand mixed with water
- **Duration:** 1 hour

When you cast this spell, you create a 10-foot-diameter pit of quicksand, sticky mud, or a similar dangerous natural hazard suited to the region. A creature that's in the area when the spell is cast or that enters the affected area must make a successful Strength saving throw or sink up to its waist and be [restrained](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Restrained) by the mire. From that point on, the mire acts as [quicksand](compendium/traps-hazards/quicksand.md), but the DC for Strength checks to escape from the quicksand is equal to your spell save DC. A creature outside the mire trying to pull another creature free receives a +5 bonus on its Strength check.
