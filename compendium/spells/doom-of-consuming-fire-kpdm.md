# Doom of Consuming Fire
*2nd-level, Evocation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a dead coal or a fistful of ashes
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You are wreathed in cold, purple fire that damages creatures near you. You take `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) cold damage each round for the duration of the spell. Creatures within 5 feet of you when you cast the spell and at the start of each of your turns while the spell is in effect take `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) cold damage.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a 3rd level spell slot, the purple fire extends 10 feet from you, you take `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) cold damage, and other creatures take `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) cold damage.

When you cast this spell using a 4th-level slot, the fire extends 15 feet from you, you take `dice:1d10|noform|avg` (`1d10`) cold damage, and other creatures take `dice:1d12|noform|avg` (`1d12`) cold damage.

When you cast this spell using a slot of 5th level or higher, the fire extends to 20 feet, you take `dice:1d12|noform|avg` (`1d12`) cold damage, and other creatures take `dice:1d20|noform|avg` (``) cold damage.
