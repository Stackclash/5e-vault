# Ray of Life Suppression
*4th-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You launch a swirling ray of disruptive energy at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the creature takes `dice:6d8|noform|avg` (`6d8`) necrotic damage and its maximum hit points are reduced by an equal amount. This reduction lasts until the creature finishes a short or long rest, or until it receives the benefit of a [greater restoration](compendium/spells/greater-restoration.md) spell or comparable magic.

This spell has no effect on constructs or undead.
