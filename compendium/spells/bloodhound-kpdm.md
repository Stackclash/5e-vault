# Bloodhound
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, a drop of ammonia
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You touch a willing creature to grant it an enhanced sense of smell. For the duration, that creature has advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on smell and on Wisdom ([Survival](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Survival)) checks to follow tracks.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you also grant the target [blindsight](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Blindsight) out to a range of 30 feet for the duration.
