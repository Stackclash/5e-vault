# Adjust Position
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You adjust the location of an ally to a better tactical position. You move one willing creature within range 5 feet. This movement does not provoke [opportunity attacks](/compendium/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack). The creature moves bodily through the intervening space (as opposed to teleporting), so there can be no physical obstacle (such as a wall or a door) in the path.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target an additional willing creature for each slot level above 1st.
