# Touch of the Unliving
*3rd-level, Necromancy*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes `dice:2d6|noform|avg` (`2d6`) necrotic damage and, if it is not an undead creature, it is [paralyzed](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed) until the end of its next turn. Until the spell ends, you can make the attack again on each of your turns as an action.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|avg|text(1d6)` for each slot level above 3rd.
