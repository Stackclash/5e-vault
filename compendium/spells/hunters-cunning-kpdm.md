# Hunter's Cunning
*2nd-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You grant a creature you touch preternatural senses and insight into its immediate environment. Until the spell ends, the target gains advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) and Wisdom ([Survival](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Survival)) checks and ignores the effect of natural (nonmagical) difficult terrain.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Druid](list-spells-classes-druid); [Ranger](list-spells-classes-ranger)

*Source: Deep Magic p. 81*
