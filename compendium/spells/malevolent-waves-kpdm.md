# Malevolent Waves
*8th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a profane object that has been bathed in blood
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You create an [invisible](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) miasma that fills the area within 30 feet of you. All your allies have advantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks they make within 30 feet of you, and all your enemies are [poisoned](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) while within that radius.

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Sorcerer](list-spells-classes-sorcerer); [Warlock](list-spells-classes-warlock); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard)
