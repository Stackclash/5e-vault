# Flurry
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a fleck of quartz
- **Duration:** 1 round

A flurry of snow surrounds you and extends to a 5-foot radius around you. While it lasts, anyone trying to see into, out of, or through the affected area (including you) has disadvantage on Wisdom ([Perception](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks and attack rolls.
