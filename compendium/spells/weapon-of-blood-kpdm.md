# Weapon of Blood
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, a pinch of iron shavings
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

When you cast this spell, you inflict `dice:1d4|noform|avg` (`1d4`) slashing damage on yourself that can't be healed until after the blade created by this spell is destroyed or the spell ends. The trickling blood transforms into a dagger of red metal that functions as a +1 dagger.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd to 5th level, the self-inflicted wound deals `dice:3d4|noform|avg` (`3d4`) slashing damage and the spell produces a +2 dagger. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th to 8th level, the self-inflicted wound deals `dice:6d4|noform|avg` (`6d4`) slashing damage and the spell produces a +2 [dagger of wounding](compendium/items/sword-of-wounding.md). When you cast this spell using a 9th-level spell slot, the self-inflicted wound deals `dice:9d4|noform|avg` (`9d4`) slashing damage and the spell produces a +3 [dagger of wounding](compendium/items/sword-of-wounding.md).
