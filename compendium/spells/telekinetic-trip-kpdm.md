# Telekinetic Trip
*cantrip, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You lash out with a burst of telekinesis, sweeping a creature within range off its feet. The target of this spell must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or fall [prone](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Prone).

**Classes**: [Bard](list-spells-classes-bard); [Sorcerer](list-spells-classes-sorcerer); [Wizard](list-spells-classes-wizard); [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](list-spells-classes-rogue-arcane-trickster)
