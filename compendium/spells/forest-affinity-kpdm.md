# Forest Affinity
*1st-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S, a raven's feather or a bit of panther fur
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

The forest floor swirls and shifts around you, welcoming you into its embrace. While in a forest, you have advantage on Dexterity ([Stealth](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks to hide. While hidden in a forest, you have advantage on your initiative rolls. The spell ends if you attack or cast a spell.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can affect one additional creature for each slot level above 1st. The spell ends if you or any other target of this spell attacks or casts a spell.
