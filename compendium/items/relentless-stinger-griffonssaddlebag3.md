# Relentless Stinger
*Weapon ([rapier](compendium/items/rapier.md)), major, rare*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag3/Relentless-Stinger.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d8 P
- **Properties**: [Finesse](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse)
- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic rapier. When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points using this weapon, the next target you hit with it before the end of your next turn takes an extra 10 piercing damage from the attack.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 3 p. 0*
