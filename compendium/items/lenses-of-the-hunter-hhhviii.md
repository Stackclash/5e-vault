# Lenses of the Hunter
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While wearing these lenses, you have [darkvision](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) to 120 feet, [truesight](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Truesight) to 30 feet, and advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks that rely on sight.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 91*
