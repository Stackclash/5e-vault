# Helm of the Minotaur
*Wondrous Item, uncommon, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

While wearing this helmet, if you move at least 10 feet straight towards a target in a turn and then make a successful [shove](/compendium/rules/actions.md#shove) attack, you may choose to deal `dice: 2d8|avg` (`2d8`) piercing damage to your target in addition to the [shove](/compendium/rules/actions.md#shove) attack's regular results.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 130*
