# Wand of Doom
*Wand, legendary (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While holding this wand, you have a +2 bonus to your spell save DC and attack bonus for spell attacks, and any necrotic damage that you deal as the result of a spell deals one additional damage dice. In addition, you may use this wand to cast the [circle of death](compendium/spells/circle-of-death.md) or [finger of death](compendium/spells/finger-of-death.md) spells once per day each.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 99*
