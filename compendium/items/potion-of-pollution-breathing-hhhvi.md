# Potion of Pollution Breathing
*Potion, rare, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

When you drink this potion, you become able to breathe in even the most polluted areas. You automatically succeed any Constitution saving throw provoked by breathing in a polluted, [poisoned](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#poisoned), or diseased air.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 137*
