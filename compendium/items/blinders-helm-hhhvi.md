# Blinders Helm
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While wearing this helmet, you gain a [blindsight](/compendium/rules/senses.md#Blindsight) out to a range of 60 feet, and are immune to the [blinded](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), and [deafened](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) conditions.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 120*
