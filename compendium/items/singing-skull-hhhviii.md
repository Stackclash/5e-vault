# Singing Skull
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew/master/_img/HHH/HHHVIII/SingingSkull.webp#right)  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While holding this item, you may cast the [speak with dead](compendium/spells/speak-with-dead.md) spell. Once used, this item cannot be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 96*
