# Potion of Poison Immunity
*Potion, rare*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

When you drink this potion, you become immune to poison damage and the [poisoned](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for the next 12 hours. This potion is green with small bubbles on the top.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 137, Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume II p. 63*
