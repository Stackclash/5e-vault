# Faerie Dragon Dust
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

You may spend an action to sprinkle some of this dust over yourself. If you do so, you magically turn [invisible](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) as if under the effect of the invisibility spell (no concentration required). This pouch contains enough dust for three sprinkles.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 127*
