# Helm of Teleportation
*Wondrous item, major, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](compendium/items/img/helm-of-teleportation.webp#right)  


This helm has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the [teleport](compendium/spells/teleport.md) spell from it. The helm regains `dice:1d3|noform|avg` (`1d3`) expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 174. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
