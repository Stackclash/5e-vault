# Harvesting Kit
*Tools*  

- **Cost**: 30 gp
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

This kit contains everything the average harvester needs to prepare and harvest a carcass for usable parts including a skinning knife, a bonesaw, 2 [glass vials](compendium/items/vial.md), [pouches](compendium/items/pouch.md) of salt, and tweezers. Proficiency with this kit lets you add your proficiency to any check made to "harvest" a creature.
