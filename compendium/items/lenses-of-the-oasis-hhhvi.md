# Lenses of the Oasis
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While wearing these lenses, you may cast the spells [disguise self](compendium/spells/disguise-self.md) and [minor illusion](compendium/spells/minor-illusion.md) at will without requiring spell components.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 132*
