# Torpor
*Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)*  

- **Properties**: [Poison](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 600 gp

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become [poisoned](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) for `dice:4d6|noform|avg` (`4d6`) hours. The [poisoned](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) creature is [incapacitated](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated).

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 258. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
