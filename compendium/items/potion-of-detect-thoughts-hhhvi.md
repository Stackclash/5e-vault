# Potion of Detect Thoughts
*Potion, uncommon, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [detect thoughts](compendium/spells/detect-thoughts.md) spell. This potion is completely clear except for a small pink blog floating in the centre of it.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 137*
