# Manticore Cloak
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

While wearing this cloak, you have advantage on Wisdom ([Survival](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Survival)) checks made to track the location of chimeras, griffons, perytons, wyverns, and dragons (true dragons only).

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 134*
