# Potion of Young Dragonstrength
*Potion, rare*  

- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)

When you drink this potion, you recover `dice:6d4+6|noform|avg` (`6d4 + 6`) hit points. In addition, you gain resistance to the damage type of the dragon it was crafted from for 1 hour.
