# Potion of Speak With Plants
*Potion, uncommon, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [speak with plants](compendium/spells/speak-with-plants.md) spell. The potion is a cloudy green with what looks like pollen floating in it.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume I p. 138*
