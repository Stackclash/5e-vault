# Squasher
*Weapon ([greatclub](compendium/items/greatclub.md)), major, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew/master/_img/TGS1/Squasher.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d8 B
- **Properties**: [Heavy](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)
- **Weight**: 10.0 lbs.

This enormous squash is strangely durable and makes for a functional magic weapon. [Druids](compendium/classes/druid.md) wielding squasher are proficient with the weapon and can use their Wisdom modifier for the weapon's attack and damage rolls instead of Strength.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 1 p. 122*
