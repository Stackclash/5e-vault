# Golden Sun Armor
*Heavy armor ([splint armor](compendium/items/splint-armor.md)), very rare (requires attunement)*  

- **Armor Class**: 17
- **Properties**: [Crafted by](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Crafted%20by)
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 60.0 lbs.

You gain a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. While wearing this armor, you may cast the [light](compendium/spells/light.md) spell on yourself at will without requiring any components. The light shed by this spell is considered to be sunlight.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume II p. 58*
