# Titan's Tooth Claymore
*Weapon ([greatsword](compendium/items/greatsword.md)), major, very rare*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag2/Items/Titans-Tooth-Claymore.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 2d6 S
- **Properties**: [Heavy](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Heavy), [Two-Handed](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Two-Handed)
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

This massive two-handed blade is made from the lost or harvested tooth of a tarrasque. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. If it hits an object, the hit is automatically a critical hit. In addition, if you roll a 20 on an attack roll using this weapon, the target takes an extra `dice:2d6|noform|avg` (`2d6`) slashing damage.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 2 p. 175*
