# Bloodthirsty Bistoury
*Weapon ([dagger](compendium/items/dagger.md)), major, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag2/Items/Bloodthirsty-Bistoury.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d4 P
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Finesse](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Light), [Thrown](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Thrown)
- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

This elegant, dark dagger is never bloodstained and effortlessly pierces flesh. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

When this dagger is used by an evil-aligned creature, any creature hit by it doesn't bleed from the attack or the wound it leaves behind for 10 minutes.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 2 p. 48*
