# Mechanus Gauntlets
*Wondrous Item, legendary, Crafted by*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

While wearing these gauntlets, you automatically succeed on any contested [Shove](/compendium/rules/actions.md#Shove) attack made against a target that is Huge or smaller. If the target is larger than that, you have advantage on your check instead. You also have a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls on melee weapon attacks.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 92*
