# Gossiper's Ear Cuff
*Wondrous item, minor, rare*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag3/Gossipes-Ear-Cuff.webp#right)  


While wearing this ear cuff, you can use an action to cast the [message](compendium/spells/message.md) cantrip from it. In addition, you automatically hear the message of any spell that targets a creature within 120 feet of you, such as by a message or [sending](compendium/spells/sending.md) spell. Messages overheard in this way sound like whispers to you, regardless of the original message.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 3 p. 0*
