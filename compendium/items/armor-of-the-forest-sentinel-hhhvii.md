# Armor of the Forest Sentinel
*Heavy Armor, rare, Crafted by (requires attunement)*  

- **Armor Class**: 17
- **Properties**: Crafted by
- **Strength**: Requires 15 STR.
- **Stealth**: The wearer has disadvantage on Stealth (DEX) checks.
- **Weight**: 60.0 lbs.

Despite being made entirely of bark, this set of armor possesses the same attributes as a normal set of [splint armor](compendium/items/splint-armor.md).

In addition, while wearing this armor, you may cast the [tree stride](compendium/spells/tree-stride.md) spell once per day without requiring any spell components or spell slots.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume II p. 54*
