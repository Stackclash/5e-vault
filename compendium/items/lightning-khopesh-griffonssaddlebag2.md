# Lightning Khopesh
*Weapon ([scimitar](compendium/items/scimitar.md)), major, rare*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheGiddyLimit/homebrew-img/main/img/GriffonsSaddlebag2/Items/Lightning-Khopesh.webp#right)  

- **Damage**: 1d6 S
- **Properties**: [Finesse](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](/compendium/rules/item-properties.md#Light)
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

This bolt-like blade crackles with electric energy. When you hit with an attack using this magic weapon, the target takes an extra `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) lightning damage. In addition, the first time you have advantage on an attack roll and miss using this weapon on each of your turns, the target takes `dice:1d6|noform|avg` (`1d6`) lightning damage as stray sparks leap from the blade to shock the target.

*Source: The Griffon's Saddlebag, Book 2 p. 115*
