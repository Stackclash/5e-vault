# Amulet of the Expanded Mind
*Wondrous Item, very rare, Crafted by (requires attunement)*  

- **Properties**: Crafted by

While wearing and attuned to this amulet, your Intelligence score increases by 2, as does your maximum for that score. In addition, any attempt to [charm](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#charmed) you or read your thoughts without your permission fails, and the creature attempting to do so suffers `dice: 2d8|avg` (`2d8`) psychic damage.

*Source: Hamund's Harvesting Handbook: Volume III p. 81*
