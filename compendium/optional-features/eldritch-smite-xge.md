# Eldritch Smite
*Source: Xanathar's Guide to Everything p. 56*  

***Prerequisites*** 5th, Pact of the Blade

Once per turn when you hit a creature with your pact weapon, you can expend a warlock spell slot to deal an extra `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) force damage to the target, plus another `dice:1d8|noform|avg` (`1d8`) per level of the spell slot, and you can knock the target [prone](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Prone) if it is Huge or smaller.
