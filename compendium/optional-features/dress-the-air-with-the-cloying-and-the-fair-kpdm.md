# Dress the Air with the Cloying and the Fair
*Source: Deep Magic p. 215*  

This symbol represents sweet scents, perfumes, Allure, and pleasant feelings. It can also be associated with influencing or changing the mind of another.

**Benefit:** You gain a +1 bonus on Charisma ([Persuasion](/compendium/rules/skills.md#Persuasion)) checks.

**Power:** If you use an action to trace this glyph on your body in cologne or Perfume, you have advantage on Charisma checks for the next hour.

**Power (3rd level)** If you use an action to trace this hieroglyph on the ground with dust or ink made from the petals of flowers, every creature within 10 feet of you must make a successful Charisma saving throw or be [charmed](/compendium/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) by you for 1 minute.

**Power (5th level)** You use an action to contemplate flower petals as they drift in the breeze, enabling you to cast a [gust of wind](compendium/spells/gust-of-wind.md) spell.

**Mastery Power:** When you use an action to anoint yourself with Perfume, for the next 10 minutes you have advantage on Charisma checks to Influence people within 50 feet of you.
